Privacy Policy.

Effective: April 24, 2026

Full Stop stores all data on your device. Nothing is sent to a server. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts. Your readings are yours alone.

What data does Full Stop collect?

Full Stop records the following information, which you provide directly:

  • Hunger and fullness level (1–10 scale)
  • Optional notes about meals and how you feel
  • Timestamps of each entry
  • Your notification and theme preferences

This is the complete list. Full Stop does not collect any other data.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device using an on-device database (SQLite). Your data never leaves your phone. There is no server, no cloud sync, and no remote database.

Does Full Stop share my data?

No. Full Stop does not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third party. There are no analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking tools integrated into the app.

Does Full Stop use analytics or tracking?

No. Full Stop does not include any analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or usage tracking. The app does not know how you use it.

Does Full Stop require an account?

No. Full Stop does not require registration, login, or any personal information. There are no accounts.

What about in-app purchases?

Full Stop offers an optional one-time purchase ("Full Stop Pro") to unlock additional features. This purchase is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). Full Stop does not receive or store any payment information.

What about notifications?

If you enable reminders, Full Stop schedules local notifications on your device. These are not push notifications—no data is sent to any server. The notification content (reminder times and messages) is generated and stored entirely on your device.

What about the CSV export?

The export feature creates a CSV file on your device and uses your phone's built-in sharing interface. Full Stop does not upload the file anywhere. Where the file goes after you share it is determined by you and the app you share it with.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. Go to Settings → Delete all data. This permanently removes all entries from your device. You can also uninstall the app, which removes all stored data.

Children's privacy

Full Stop does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app does not collect personal information from any user.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date. Since Full Stop does not collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact privacy@andrewhan.dev.
