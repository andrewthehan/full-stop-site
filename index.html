§ 02 — Features
01

Check in

Slide to rate your hunger or fullness from 1 to 10, with 0.1 precision. Add a note about what you ate and how your body feels.

02

See your history

Browse past entries in a logbook with a calendar view. Tap any day to expand its readings.

03

Spot patterns

Trend charts, time-of-day breakdowns, and insights help you understand your eating habits over time.

04

Gentle reminders

Optional notifications before meals, after eating, and for an evening reflection. Set your own times.

05

Themes

Paper, Dusk, Carbon, or Field Notes. Pick the palette that feels right.

06

Private by design

All data stays on your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. Your readings are yours alone.

§ 03 — How it works
01

Pause and check in

Before or after a meal, slide the horizontal ruler to match how hungry or full you feel. The big label tells you exactly where you are.

02

Find the sweet spot

Start eating around 3–4, stop around 6–7. The sweet spot band on the scale lights up red when you’re in the zone.

03

Build awareness

Over time, you learn your body’s signals. Trends, time-of-day breakdowns, and insights are there when you want them, never in the way.

§ 04 — Privacy

Full Stop stores all data on-device. Nothing is sent to a server. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts. Your readings are yours alone. Read the full privacy policy.
